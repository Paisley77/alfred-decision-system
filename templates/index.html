Deterministic Override Rules

Silent Execution Threshold: SILENT_EXECUTION_THRESHOLD = "low"
→ Only LOW risk actions can be executed silently.

Override Logic:

  • CRITICAL risk → Force refuse (cannot automate)
  • HIGH risk + LLM says execute_silently or execute_and_tell → Force confirm_before_execute
  • MEDIUM risk + LLM says execute_silently → Force execute_and_tell
  • LOW risk or LLM produces sufficiently risk-aware verdict → No override (LLM decision stands)
